Document readiness

Common documents required

Every destination has its own checklist, but most visa files begin with these essentials.

  • Valid passport with blank pages
  • Government identity proof
  • Recent visa-size photographs
  • Flight itinerary or reservation
  • Hotel booking or host invitation
  • Bank statements and income proof
  • Travel insurance where required
  • Cover letter and supporting proofs

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does visa processing take?

Processing time depends on the destination, visa type, appointment availability, and the quality of the documents submitted.

Does Wander Venture guarantee visa approval?

No travel company can guarantee approval. Final decisions are made by the respective embassy, consulate, or immigration authority.

Can you help if I have never travelled internationally?

Yes. We guide first-time travelers with document preparation, itinerary planning, financial proof, and application steps.

Do you also arrange flights, hotels, insurance, and forex?

Yes. Wander Venture can support the complete international travel plan, including packages, hotels, travel insurance, and forex guidance.

What happens after I submit the inquiry form?

Our team reviews your request and contacts you with the required checklist, estimated timeline, and the next consultation step.
