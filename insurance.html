FAQ

Insurance questions

Is travel insurance mandatory?

Some destinations and visa categories require insurance. Others make it optional but highly recommended.

Can families get one plan?

Family and group options may be available depending on traveler ages, destination, and duration.

Do you settle claims?

Claims are handled by the insurer. Wander Venture can guide travelers on documentation and process awareness.
